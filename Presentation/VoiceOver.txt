Slide 1 – Cover Page

Hello everyone. My name is Atanu Banerjee from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Techno India University. In this presentation, I will be introducing MindEase, a Student Mental Wellness Platform developed in alignment with SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being.

Slide 2 – SDG Identification

SDG 3 focuses on ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all. Our project specifically addresses student mental health by helping individuals monitor stress, improve emotional awareness, and maintain a healthier lifestyle.

Slide 3 – Problem Definition

Today, many students face academic pressure, stress, anxiety, and burnout. These challenges often affect their concentration, productivity, and overall well-being. Unfortunately, there are very few simple and accessible tools that help students regularly monitor their mental health.

Slide 4 – Proposed Solution

To address this issue, we developed MindEase, a student-focused mental wellness platform. It allows users to track their mood, assess stress levels, monitor healthy habits, receive personalized recommendations, and access mental health support resources.

Slide 5 – Key Features

The main features of MindEase include a Mood Tracker for recording daily emotions, a Stress Assessment Quiz for identifying stress levels, a Habit Tracker for monitoring healthy activities, a Wellness Recommendation Engine, a Motivation Center, and an Emergency Support section.

Slide 6 – System Workflow

The workflow is simple and user-friendly. Students first provide their mood and stress-related information. The system then analyzes the data and generates personalized recommendations, helping users improve their mental well-being through regular monitoring and healthy habits.

Slide 7 – Live Demo

Now I will briefly demonstrate the platform. The demo includes user registration and login, mood tracking, stress assessment, wellness recommendations, habit tracking, and the emergency support section.

Slide 8 – Tech Stack & Feasibility Analysis

MindEase is developed using React, Tailwind CSS, FastAPI, and Local Storage. The project is technically feasible, cost-effective, and easy to deploy. Since it uses open-source technologies, it requires minimal resources while providing an efficient user experience.

Slide 9 – Innovation Factor

What makes MindEase unique is that it combines multiple wellness tools into a single platform specifically designed for students. By integrating mood tracking, stress assessment, habit monitoring, and personalized recommendations, it provides a comprehensive approach to mental wellness.

Slide 10 – Impact & Future Scope

MindEase can help students identify stress at an early stage, improve emotional awareness, and develop healthier daily habits. In the future, the platform can be enhanced with AI-powered counseling, emotion detection, therapist integration, and mobile application support.
